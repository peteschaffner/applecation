Create your own policy brief

We have created a template and a set of guidelines to help your organization create a Stronger Nation-like policy brief for your own county or region.

Style guide

The Stronger Nation report has a unique graphic style that is both visually appealing and easily legible. You are encouraged, when drafting your own Stronger Nation brief, to adhere to this style guide.

Typography

  • For headings, drop caps, charts, graphs and tables, use Gill Sans. If unavailable, please use Verdana as a sans serif replacement.
  • For body copy, use Baskerville if available, and Times New Roman if not.
  • In general, follow the typographic standards and patterns set in the InDesign document you download.

Pages

The policy brief template is designed to fit on two pages, front and back.
